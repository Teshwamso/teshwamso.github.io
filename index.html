Projects

Chama Smart

Chama Smart is an intuitive and user-friendly platform designed to streamline group financial management and goal tracking. It empowers users to create, organize, and manage savings groups efficiently, facilitating collaborative financial planning and transparent tracking of group objectives. With features for goal setting, member management, transaction tracking, and reporting.

Pillars Digital

As the co-founder of Pillars Digital, our brand is dedicated to fostering growth for other businesses. We specialize in social media management, digital content creation, digital media strategies, website development and IT solutions for companies seeking to expand their online presence and reach.

East Cooperative society

As the co-founder of East Cooperative Society, our mission is to empower and mobilize individuals, particularly youth like ourselves, towards financial empowerment, community service, and self-sufficiency. We aim to foster financial savings, provide access to loans, and encourage active participation in community development initiatives

Tesh Faundation

I founded Tesh Foundation as a means of serving both God and society. I strongly believe in the importance of giving back to the less fortunate. Every year, we organize events in children's homes, jails, hospitals, and on the streets, aiming to provide support and uplift the spirits of those in need. My primary future goal is to establish a home where the less fortunate, especially children, can access high-quality services and care, ensuring they receive the support they deserve.
